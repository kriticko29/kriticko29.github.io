Detection Engineer with extensive experience in identifying, analyzing, and mitigating advanced cyber threats across enterprise environments. Specializing in threat detection engineering, reverse engineering, and operationalizing threat intelligence to build high-fidelity detections mapped to MITRE ATT&CK. Skilled in developing and tuning KQL-based detection rules for Microsoft Defender for Endpoint (MDE) and Azure Sentinel, as well as leveraging tools like Splunk, YARA, IDA Pro, and Wireshark to uncover malicious behavior. Adept at system integrity validation, IOC development, and threat hunting across endpoint, network, and cloud telemetry. Strong background in scripting with Python, PowerShell, and Bash, with a focus on detection-as-code, alert quality, and reducing false positives. Passionate about enabling SOC and IR teams with actionable detections that defend against evolving attacker tactics and techniques.
